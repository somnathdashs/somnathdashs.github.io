Auto Chat AI – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

1. Overview

Auto Chat AI is an intelligent assistant app designed to help users automate replies using AI (Gemini API) for WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data remains secure and private.

2. Information We Collect

• Notification Content: We access notifications from WhatsApp only to read incoming messages and generate smart replies.
• No Personal Storage on Web: We do not upload or store any information on cloud servers or the internet.
• Local-Only Storage: All data, including message context and AI interaction history, remains strictly on your device.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the notification content only to:
• Generate relevant AI-powered replies using Gemini API.
• Help you respond to personal or business messages on WhatsApp efficiently.

4. Data Storage and Sharing

• No data is stored on our servers or any cloud system.
• All processing and response generation happens locally or via secure API calls directly from your device.
• We never share your information with third parties.

5. Permissions Used

• Notification Access: To read messages from WhatsApp.
• Internet Access: Required only to interact with the Gemini AI API securely.

6. Security

Your data stays on your phone. We don’t track, analyze, or upload anything externally. All actions are initiated and handled by your device securely.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to our privacy practices, we will update this policy accordingly and inform users where appropriate.
